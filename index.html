BARBIE

A home automation project where the user's appliances are controlled by his voice.

An AI based smart home automation system that recognises your voice using NLP and then interprets that voice to convert it into text and then sends that request to the server to turn on the required appliances in the home present. The appliances are controlled using the micro-controller or a microprocessor.

The project was developed over Android and iOS platforms using an Arduino and python for making the NLP model.This project won a consolation prize in the ISGW SmartGrid Hackathon.

The Smart Bot in the mobile app is as good as any market-selling bot such as Siri or alexa.

Beagos

An that determines whether a product is real or duplicate.

An app that has a use in all the marketplaces , each shop in the market is provided by beacons (A beacon is bluetooth low emitting device that is used for sending and recieving data through other bluetooth devices), whenever a user has his app open it is connected to that shop's beacon which is nearest to it and all the details of that shop are displayed to the user and the user can choose any of the items that are sold in the shop manually by picking it from his hand and then scans that item using the app when the image is scanned it is worked upon a classifier that determines whether the logo on the app is authentic or not and in such a manner a rating is produced from the app and this rating is stored in the beacon and the next time another customer that repeats the same process rates the shop automatically.

The app is developed on Android platform and soon will be developed on the iOS platform too.

MedEx

A platform to connect all the medicine buyers and sellers.

MedEx a platform that connects all the medicine buyers and sellers.

This platform provides a common page for registration of the sellers and buyers, Sellers may post images of the medicine that is of no further use to them along with other details that are necessary to distinguish a medicine such as :

The quantity of the medicine, the expiry date of the medicine and the price in which the seller wants to sell the medicine.

An api is used in the app that fetches all the additional information about the medicine once its basic details are entered. The portal for the buyers consists of a search feature that automatically searches all the medicine corresponding to the name of the entered medicine with also combining the location parameter in the results such that the location that is nearest to the person's address is shown first and likewise.

The project is developed over Android and Web Platforms and using PHP as the backend.
